The Parkview in Pleasanton 

Read about how Re-Mind members Pranav and Srikar visit the Parkview to host a concert for the seniors living in the Parkview. 



Sunrise Senior Living in San Ramon

ReMind’s first event with Sunrise Senior Living in San Ramon was a huge success. Performers Pranav Ramesh and Srikar Bhuvan collaborated to deliver a truly special event for the seniors at this location.

Brookdale Senior Living in North Fremont

On August 24, 2019, ReMind was able to partner up with Brookdale Senior Living of North Fremont.



Oak Creek Alzheimer's and Dementia care in Castro Valley


 ReMind carried on its series of successful music concerts for senior living with dementia in a new memory care home in                                Castro Valley.               

Pacific Senior Living in Union City

ReMind jump-started this new event series at Pacifica Senior Living in Union City. Popular songs from the Beatles, Dean Martin, and Frank Sinatra were all sung while also incorporating some popular American Folk Songs.

On Lok Life Way

A successful music therapy event was held in On Lok Lifeways, a senior residential community in San Jose. Music therapists Taffy Su and Sylvester Sroka joined us again in providing a spectacular afternoon music therapy session for seniors.
